Safety Guideline

Along with the uniqueness of grilling your delicious food at your table, there are some safety precautions to keep in mind!

  • Please do NOT touch any of the grill parts on the table! They can be VERY HOT.
  • If the flame comes up from the grill, please lean back and wait for staff assistance. Do NOT attempt to extinguish the flame on your own.
  • Sometimes our grill may "pop" due to chemical reaction with heat. If you hear any "popping", please lean away and look away from the grill and seek assistance.

*GROUP OF 6 OR MORE PEOPLE WILL HAVE 20% GROUP SERVICE CHARGE
**CONSUMER ADVISORY: consuming raw or undercooked meats, poultry, seafood, shellfish, or eggs may increase your risk of foodborne illness. Some items contain raw/undercooked ingredients. Please consult your server for more information.
***ONE TYPE OF MEAT SHOULD BE COOKED ON THE GRILL AT A TIME TO PREVENT CROSS CONTAMINATION. THERE IS A CHARGE FOR EXCESSIVE LEFTOVER FOOD
