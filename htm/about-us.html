ABOUT US

Our Family

Our managers strive to provide a safe, friendly, loving, family-like working environment for all our employees, regardless of their history, background, culture, ethnicity, and more.

We consider each staff member to be a family member than a mere employee, while maintaining a good level of work professionalism.

We sincerely believe that happy staff will provide a genuine, honest and wonderful service to our valued customers.

Our History

Iron Age first opened as a small, tiny restaurant in Duluth, Georgia. We specialized in pork belly and brisket and had less than 10 tables !

We decided to incorporate Korean music and cuisine by integrating Korean marinade into different types of meats to expand our menu.

Today, we have many locations in several states and internationally!

We strive to keep growing and expanding as we introduce more and more people to Korean cuisine, Korean BBQ, and Korean music
